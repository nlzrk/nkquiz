question file format

Multiple choice — asterisk marks the correct answer:

{type:MC, Q:What is 2+2?, A:a;3 *b;4 c;5 d;6}

Short answer — sample answer always shown, M sets marks available (default 5):

{type:SA, Q:Capital of France?, A:Paris, M:1}

Question bank — pick N random questions from the file:

{bank:10}

{type:MC, Q:...}
{type:SA, Q:...}
... as many questions as you like

LaTeX in questions:

{type:MC, Q:Solve $x^2=9$, A:a;x=2 *b;x=±3 c;x=4 d;x=9}

Images in questions:

{type:SA, Q:Name this. {img:./img.png}, A:Carbon, M:1}
